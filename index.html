Our Story

More Than a Café

Solly's Café & Car Wash is the heartbeat of Louis Trichardt. Born from a simple vision we make every journey memorable as we've grown into a full lifestyle destination where community, cuisine, and clean cars go hand in hand.

What We Offer

Our Services

Café & Dining

Enjoy a curated menu of freshly prepared meals, light bites, and beverages in our beautiful outdoor and indoor spaces. From morning coffee to evening sundowners.

Car Wash

Professional car wash services while you sit back and relax in our café. Interior, exterior, full valet — we make your ride shine as bright as your day.

Events & Parties

Host your birthday, corporate function, year-end, or any celebration at Solly's. We handle décor, catering, sound, and entertainment so you can enjoy the moment.

Catering & Buffets

Lavish buffet spreads with salads, grilled meats, fruit platters, and more. Our catering team delivers luxury presentation and incredible flavour every time.

Live Entertainment

From DJs to live performers, we bring the energy. Solly's is the go-to venue for unforgettable nights with top local and regional artists.

VIP Lounge

Our premium lounge area features plush seating, a bar, and an exclusive atmosphere. Perfect for those who want to celebrate in style with bottle service and more.

Celebrations
